Frequently asked questions

How do I create an account?

Download the Credible app from the App Store or Google Play, then create an account with your email address and mobile phone number. A payment method is also needed before you can ride.

How do I request a ride?

When you 're ready to go, open the app and enter your destination. Then choose the ride option that best suits your needs. Confirm your pickup by tapping Confirm Pickup.

Is Credible available in my city?

You can find Credible only in Cairo.

Can I use Credible without a smartphone?

Yes, you can request a ride by signing in to B2BUrbanMobility.com.
